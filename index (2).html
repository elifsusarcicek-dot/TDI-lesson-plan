🔍 CONCEPT QUESTIONS — click to reveal answers
Owen says "I packed a chicken." Can he have one chicken? Yes — it's a whole bird. Countable.
DJ says "I brought some chicken." Can he have one chicken here? No — it's loose meat for an animal. Uncountable.
Gwen has "a sketchbook" AND "some paper." What changes between the two? One is a countable object, the other is an uncountable material.
